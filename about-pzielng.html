Our story

PZIEL was established in 2018 by ALubankudi Idris Akorede (A Professional graphic Designer, Web Designer, Publsiher & Consultor).

PZIEL is a Digital Printing & Branding Company with professional staffs & adequate equipments. The Brand has worked with many companies, And the first-class leading comapanies in africa today are the top clients of our Best Practices. The company (PZIEL) is located in Lagos (The third most populous city in Africa), Nigeria.

Our digital printing company specializes in producing high-quality printing materials for businesses of all sizes. We use state-of-the-art equipment to produce vivid and vibrant prints that are sure to make a lasting impression on your clients and customers. Whether you need business cards, brochures, flyers, or more, we can work with you to create a customized design that represents your brand and stands out from the competition. Our team of skilled designers is ready to work with you to create a custom design that meets your needs and exceeds your expectations. We pride ourselves on offering outstanding customer service, using only the highest quality materials, and delivering your prints on time, every time.

Contact us today to learn how we can help take your business to the next level with top-notch digital printing services.

We do this across:

  • Design: The first step is to create a design for the printed material. Our creative designers are available to give you topnotch design and bring your ideas to life. We use various software tools to create digital designs that are optimized for printing.
  • Pre-press: Once the design is finalized, We move into the pre-press stage. Here, we make sure that the design is properly formatted and ready for printing. Colors, Images, and we ensure that the design will print correctly on the chosen material.
  • Printing: This is the main stage of the process. We use various printing technologies to produce high-quality prints on various types of paper or materials. Mostly offset and digital.
  • Post-press:After the initial printing, various finishing touches are needed to give the printed material a professional look. This may include cutting, folding, binding, laminating, or coating. at PZIEL, We use perfect machines and tools to perform these finishing touches.
  • Packaging and delivery: The last stage is packaging and delivery of the final printed product to our clients. We ensure that the printed material is packaged securely and delivered on time to the clients desired address.
